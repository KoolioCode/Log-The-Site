Square Up

Overview

Enter the fast-paced, adrenaline-pumping world of Square Up! Developed by Office Chair Studios, Square Up is an endless dodging game that challenges your reflexes, strategic thinking, and endurance. As a nimble white square, your mission is simple: survive against relentless waves of enemies and achieve the highest time possible.

Intuitive Controls

Move your square with each click, allowing for precise dodging and quick maneuvers.

Endless Waves

Face an infinite array of enemies that get progressively harder, ensuring no two runs are ever the same.

High Score System

Compete against yourself and others by aiming for the highest survival time. The longer you last, the higher your score.

Dynamic Soundtrack

Immerse yourself in an energetic soundtrack that intensifies as the game progresses.
